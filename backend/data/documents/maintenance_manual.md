# Preventive Maintenance Manual

## Overview
This manual covers preventive maintenance schedules and procedures for all major HVAC equipment in the building management system.

## Air Handling Units (AHU)

### Monthly Tasks
- Check and replace air filters (MERV-13)
- Inspect fan belts for wear and tension
- Check bearing lubrication
- Verify VFD operation and parameters
- Clean condensate drain pan and trap
- Check damper actuator operation

### Quarterly Tasks
- Clean cooling and heating coils
- Inspect ductwork for leaks
- Test smoke detectors and fire dampers
- Calibrate temperature and pressure sensors
- Check economizer operation
- Verify outdoor air minimum settings

### Annual Tasks
- Complete motor inspection and megger testing
- Fan wheel cleaning and balancing
- Full control sequence verification
- Coil pressure testing
- Complete lubrication of all bearings
- VFD parameter backup and verification

## Chillers

### Weekly Tasks
- Log operating pressures and temperatures
- Check oil level and condition
- Monitor compressor amperage
- Verify condenser water flow
- Check for unusual vibration or noise

### Monthly Tasks
- Analyze refrigerant for moisture and acid
- Check starter and electrical connections
- Inspect and clean strainers
- Verify safety control operation
- Review trending data for anomalies

### Annual Tasks
- Eddy current testing of condenser tubes
- Compressor oil analysis
- Vibration analysis
- Refrigerant leak detection survey
- Full controls calibration
- Complete electrical inspection

## Boilers

### Daily Tasks
- Check water level and pressure
- Verify flame pattern
- Monitor stack temperature
- Check for leaks

### Monthly Tasks
- Test low water cutoff
- Inspect burner components
- Check combustion efficiency
- Test safety valves
- Inspect refractory

### Annual Tasks
- Complete internal inspection
- Hydrostatic testing
- Burner tuning and combustion analysis
- Water treatment system evaluation
- Complete electrical inspection
- Update certification documentation

## Cooling Towers

### Weekly Tasks
- Check water treatment chemistry
- Inspect fill media condition
- Verify fan operation
- Check basin water level
- Inspect drift eliminators

### Monthly Tasks
- Clean strainers and screens
- Check belt tension (belt-drive fans)
- Inspect bearings and lubrication
- Verify water treatment dosing
- Check vibration levels

### Seasonal Tasks
- Complete cleaning and disinfection (spring startup)
- Winterization procedure (fall shutdown)
- Fill media inspection and replacement as needed
- Motor and drive inspection
- Complete structural inspection
